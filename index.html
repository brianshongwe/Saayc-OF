 CREDO:

 

 A driving force for young pioneering spirits.

 

 

 PROGRAMMES:

 

 ICTAGE:

Southern African Association of Youth Club(SAAYC) implement comprehensive computer literacy programme aimed at giving young people skills to use computer technology everyday life to develop new social and economic opportunities for themself,their family,and their communities.

 

 LIFE SKILLS:

The SAAYC launch programme is primarily developed to anchor beneficaries from centers to br fully capacitated in Financial Literacy,Interpersonal skills,Job readines,Leadership skills, Civic Engagement (Active citizenship) and personal development it address the needs of teenagers & young adults and youth around the Saayc center who seek to learn basic essential life skills.

 

  EDUCATIONAL SUPPORT:

Complements and builds on the efforts done by the Department of Education in assisting grade 9 learners in choosing subjects in line with their dream careers and improving their literacy rate at a critical stage in their life.
